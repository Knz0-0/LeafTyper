# Leaf Typer

Leaf Typer is a fast-paced arcade game where reflexes, precision, and rhythm are everything.

Leaves fall from the sky, each labeled with a letter.  
Your goal is simple: press the correct key to slash them before they hit the ground.

Sounds easy? It isn’t.

As your combo increases, the game becomes more intense:
- Faster falling leaves  
- Increasing spawn rate  
- More complex inputs  
- Stronger visual and audio feedback  

The better you play, the harder the game pushes back.

---

## Core Gameplay

- Type the correct key to destroy falling leaves  
- Build combos to increase your score  
- Missing a key resets your combo  
- Survive as long as possible  

---

## Features

- Dynamic difficulty scaling based on time and performance  
- Combo-based feedback (visual effects, sound, hit freeze)  
- Multiple unlockable skins  
- Persistent progression (coins, unlocked skins, equipped skin)  
- Smooth transitions and polished UI  
- Procedural elements and responsive controls  

---

## Progression System

Earn coins based on your score and unlock new skins:

- Basic skins available early  
- Advanced skins require higher performance  
- Cosmetic-only rewards focused on style and mastery  

---

## Controls

| Key | Action |
|-----|------|
| A–Z | Slash matching leaf |
| Mouse | Navigate menus |

---
